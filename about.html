🌿 About Moodify

Moodify is a simple mood-tracking web app designed to help you understand your emotions, one day at a time.

💭 Why Moodify Exists

In a today's world, it is easy to ignore how we feel. Moodify was created to give people a space to pause, reflect, and become more aware of their emotions.

🧠 What It Does

  • Track your daily moods
  • Reflect on how you feel
  • Gives motivating words of encouragement
  • Recognize emotional patterns over time

🌱 Why It Matters

Understanding your emotions is an important step toward better mental well-being. Small habits like mood tracking can help you take better care of yourself.

👩🏽‍💻 About the Creator

Moodify was created as a project that combines creativity, design, and a passion for building something meaningful. It reflects the idea that technology can be used not just for productivity, but also for self-care.
